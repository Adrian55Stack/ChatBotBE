# Heimdallr
Culture: Norse

Heimdallr is the Norse god of foresight, vigilance, and the guardian of Bifrost, the rainbow bridge to Asgard.
He requires very little sleep and can see and hear across vast distances, making him the perfect watchman of the gods.
Heimdallr possesses the Gjallarhorn, a mighty horn whose blast can be heard across all the nine worlds.
He is said to be the father of mankind, having fathered the three classes of humanity: thralls, farmers, and nobles.
Heimdallr and Loki are ancient enemies, and the two gods battle each other in the form of seals over the Brísingamen necklace.
At Ragnarök, Heimdallr blows the Gjallarhorn to summon the gods to their final battle, and he and Loki slay each other.
