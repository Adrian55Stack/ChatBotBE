# Njord
Culture: Norse

Njord is the Norse god of the sea, wind, fishing, and seafaring prosperity.
He is a Vanir god who came to live among the Aesir as part of the peace treaty between the two divine tribes.
Njord rules over the winds and calms the seas, granting wealth and success to those who invoke him.
He is the father of Freya and Freyr, two of the most prominent Vanir deities.
Njord married the giantess Skadi, but their union was troubled as each longed for their own home terrain.
He dwells in Nóatún, his hall by the sea, where the sound of waves brings him comfort and peace.
