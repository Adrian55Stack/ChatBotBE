# Frigg
Culture: Norse

Frigg is the queen of Asgard, wife of Odin, and goddess of marriage, motherhood, and foresight.
She is the highest ranking of the Aesir goddesses and sits beside Odin on his throne Hlidskjalf.
Frigg possesses the gift of prophecy, knowing the fates of all beings, though she rarely speaks of them.
She is the mother of Baldr and desperately sought to protect him by asking all things to swear not to harm him.
Her efforts were undone when Loki discovered she had overlooked the mistletoe.
Frigg is associated with the spinning of clouds and the domestic arts of weaving.
