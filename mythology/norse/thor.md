# Thor
Culture: Norse

Thor is the Norse god of thunder, storms, strength, and protection.
He is the son of Odin and Jörð, and one of the most widely worshipped Norse gods.
His weapon is the hammer Mjölnir, forged by the dwarven smiths Sindri and Brokkr.
Thor is one of the chief defenders of Asgard and frequently battles giants and serpents.
He rides across the sky in a chariot pulled by two goats, Tanngrisnir and Tanngnjóstr.
During Ragnarök, Thor fights Jörmungandr and slays the Midgard Serpent, but dies from its venom.
