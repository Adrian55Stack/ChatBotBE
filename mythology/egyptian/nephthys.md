# Nephthys
Culture: Egyptian

Nephthys is the Egyptian goddess of mourning, night, death, and service to the dead.
She is the daughter of Geb and Nut, sister of Isis and Osiris, and wife of Seth.
Despite being married to Seth, Nephthys sided with Isis after Seth murdered Osiris.
She helped Isis gather the body parts of Osiris and participated in the rituals to resurrect him.
Nephthys and Isis together served as divine mourners and protectors of the dead and their tombs.
She is depicted as a woman wearing a headdress in the shape of a house with a basket on top, representing her name.
