# Seth
Culture: Egyptian

Seth is the Egyptian god of chaos, storms, the desert, violence, and foreigners.
He is the son of Geb and Nut, and the brother of Osiris, Isis, and Nephthys.
Consumed by envy of Osiris's power, Seth murdered his brother and dismembered his body.
Seth and Horus engaged in a legendary series of contests and battles over the rulership of Egypt.
Despite his destructive nature, Seth played a vital protective role aboard Ra's solar barque, defending it against the serpent Apophis.
He is depicted with the head of an unidentified animal with a curved snout and squared ears, known as the Set animal.
