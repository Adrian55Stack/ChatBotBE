# Osiris
Culture: Egyptian

Osiris is the Egyptian god of the afterlife, death, resurrection, and agriculture.
He was once a great king of Egypt who taught humanity the arts of civilization and agriculture.
His brother Set, consumed by jealousy, murdered Osiris and scattered his body parts across Egypt.
Isis, his devoted wife, gathered his remains and resurrected him long enough to conceive their son Horus.
Osiris descended to the underworld to become its ruler and judge of the dead.
In the Hall of Two Truths, Osiris presides over the weighing of the heart, determining the fate of souls in the afterlife.
