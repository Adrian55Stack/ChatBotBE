# Demeter
Culture: Greek

Demeter is the Greek goddess of the harvest, grain, agriculture, and the fertility of the earth.
She is the daughter of the Titans Cronus and Rhea, and one of the twelve Olympians.
Demeter is the mother of Persephone, whose abduction by Hades caused her immeasurable grief.
While searching for Persephone, Demeter neglected the earth, causing crops to wither and famine to spread across the world.
Zeus intervened and negotiated Persephone's partial return, establishing the cycle of the seasons.
Demeter is associated with the Eleusinian Mysteries, ancient religious rites promising initiates a blessed afterlife.
