# Zeus
Culture: Greek

Zeus is the king of the Olympian gods, ruler of Mount Olympus, and god of the sky, thunder, and justice.
He is the youngest son of the Titans Cronus and Rhea, and overthrew his father to become the supreme deity.
Zeus wields the thunderbolt as his weapon, crafted for him by the Cyclopes during the Titanomachy.
He is known for his numerous affairs with goddesses and mortal women, fathering many gods and heroes.
Zeus governs the laws of hospitality and justice, punishing those who break oaths or mistreat guests.
Despite his supreme power, Zeus himself is subject to the decrees of the Fates, the Moirai, who govern destiny.
