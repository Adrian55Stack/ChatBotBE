# Cronus
Culture: Greek

Cronus is the leader of the Titans and the ruler of the cosmos before the rise of the Olympian gods.
He is the son of Uranus and Gaia, and overthrew his father by castrating him with an adamantine sickle.
A prophecy foretold that Cronus would be overthrown by one of his own children, so he swallowed each one at birth.
His wife Rhea deceived him when Zeus was born, hiding the infant and offering Cronus a stone wrapped in cloth instead.
Zeus grew to adulthood, forced Cronus to disgorge his siblings, and led the Olympians in a ten-year war against the Titans.
After his defeat in the Titanomachy, Cronus was imprisoned in Tartarus, the deepest abyss of the underworld.
