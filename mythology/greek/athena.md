# Athena
Culture: Greek

Athena is the Greek goddess of wisdom, warfare strategy, crafts, and civilization.
She was born fully grown and armored from the forehead of Zeus after he swallowed her mother Metis.
Athena is the patron goddess of Athens, having won the city's devotion by gifting it the olive tree.
She is a warrior goddess but favors strategic warfare over the raw brutality embodied by Ares.
Athena aided many Greek heroes, including Odysseus, Perseus, and Heracles, guiding them with wisdom and cunning.
She is depicted wearing a helmet and aegis, accompanied by her sacred owl, symbol of wisdom and knowledge.
