# Apollo
Culture: Greek

Apollo is the Greek god of the sun, light, music, poetry, prophecy, truth, archery, and healing.
He is the son of Zeus and the Titaness Leto, and the twin brother of Artemis, goddess of the hunt.
Apollo presides over the Oracle at Delphi, through which he communicated divine prophecy to mortals.
He is depicted as an ideally beautiful young man, often holding a lyre or a bow and arrow.
Apollo brought both plague and healing, and his arrows could strike down mortals with disease.
He is also the leader of the Muses and patron of the arts, embodying the Greek ideal of harmonious excellence.
